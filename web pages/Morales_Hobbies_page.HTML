Janine enjoys reading avidly as well as cooking for her family and students as she learns new recipes and studies new cultures and their flavors. A lover of nature, Janine has been attempting to spend more time that she already does exploring and enjoying nature as she goes to parks, beaches and anywhere else she can visit to collect sea shells, inspect the wild life and enjoy the sun. Her children have many fond memories of the nature trail walks during summer that got them out of the house and exploring as they enjoyed nature alongside her as well as visiting local museums to learn more about the local history of Florida as well as the sea life. Janine has never strayed away from experimenting with food and cooking things outside of her usual scope. She also has experimented with food for the sake of education as she once created hardtack authentically in the way that it would have been prepared for military use to show the eating situation during the time. Although this is a less than desirable form of food for the sake of education, Janine also spends her time cooking and prepping much more savory foods than Hardtack for her students as shown by her Greek salad prep image below. With her mother being an avid cook who has always made delicious meals for her and her grandchildren, Janine followed in her footsteps as she creates decadent meals to this day for her family and far beyond.
